The Orchestra Earned Media Diagnostic System

Powered by ReputeAI

What it is

A structured, article-level analysis that assigns States and Modifiers to topics, narratives, and entities to quantify positioning, narrative control, and reputational impact.

Why it matters

Identify leadership openings, under-leveraged positives, and risk vectors early. Focus comms on frames that compound momentum while mitigating reputational drag.

Measurement Scales

  • Prominence: 0–5
  • Sentiment: -4 to +4 (weak sentiment collapsed)
  • Outlet Quality: 1–5

State → Modifier

Each unit receives exactly one State, then one Modifier (first match by precedence).

Outputs

  • Topic, Narrative, and Entity states
  • Entity modifiers with precedence
  • Validation and counts
